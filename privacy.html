Privacy Policy

Last updated:

MartinMade3D Assistant (“the App”) is an internal tool used only by MartinMade3D staff.

Data we access

  • Etsy conversation threads and limited order metadata required for customer support.

How we use data

  • Generate summaries of message history.
  • Identify the most recent buyer question and draft suggested replies for human review.

What we don’t do

  • No public access, no sharing or selling of data.
  • No automatic messaging, listing edits, or price changes.

Retention & deletion

  • Support summaries and drafts may be stored in our internal systems for up to 90 days, then deleted or anonymized.
  • You may request deletion of conversation-derived data by emailing us.

Security

Access is limited to staff accounts. Credentials and API keys are stored outside of the public repository.
